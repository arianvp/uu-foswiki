The combinators in the pretty-printing library are optimal in the sense that they produce the layout with the smallest height possible within a given width. Special combinators allow the sepcification of alternative layouts.

Authors: [[http://www.memi.umss.edu.bo/~pablo/][Pablo Azero]] and Main.DoaitseSwierstra
